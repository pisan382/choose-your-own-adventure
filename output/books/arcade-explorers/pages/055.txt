He blinks his one eve open and closed. You ask, "Are
you okay?"
Vidi doesn't reply. Suddenly a beam of light shoots out
of his eye, lighting up the tunnel ahead. "Not bad!" you
tell him.
"Ready to go are we," Vidi says. The Smole scientist
gives you a gentle shove, and you both go sliding into the
mountain tunnel.
With your legs out straight and your arms at your side,
you are carried swiftly through the slippery twists and
turns of the passageway. By Vidi's light you see that the
tunnel floor is smooth and polished. The roof, however,
is covered with gleaming jewels of every kind.
You pick up speed then suddenly shoot out into the air
and tumble upside down! You land in a pile of thick red
grass. Vidi falls off your shoulder and rolls across the
grass.
