What the Program Does
In order to receive your mission instruc-
tions from ACT, you must decode their
scrambled message. This program decodes the
message. However, the earthquake disrupted
communications before the transmission was
complete, so you get only part of the mes-
sage.
How the Program Works

letter has been encoded using an odd/even al-
