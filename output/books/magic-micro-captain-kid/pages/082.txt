About the program:
The parrot told you to wash the map.
Now “X” marks the spot — and the spot
. . . and the spot. . . . But which one is the
treasure really hidden under? The friendly
parrot tells you it’s the one that will make
him fly. Try feeding him the numbers be­
side each “X on the map. Only one will
make him leave his perch. You can simply
keep trying numbers until you find the
right one. Or, if you look at line 90 of the
program, you’ll see that it’s the one that’s
divisible by 7.
