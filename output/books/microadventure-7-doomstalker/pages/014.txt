There's silence around the table as the ACT
members picture that horrible finale for the hu-
man race.
Frieda recovers first. "Why haven't we
heard about this? Has there been a cover-up?"
Suddenly you see the analytical mind beneath
that soft-looking exterior.
"You could call it a cover-up," the gen-
eral admits reluctantly. "But what good would
it have done to let the public know that the earth
seemed to be set for certain destruction?"
"You wouldn't have flown this team
here," Arch interjects, "if the situation were
absolutely hopeless."
"Correct. There is a slim chance," the
general concedes. "And this ACT team is it."
He lets that sink in and then continues.
