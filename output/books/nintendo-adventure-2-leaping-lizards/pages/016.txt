Morton Koopa, a large, armored turtle, is lum-
bering around the chamber. As Luigi watches
in alarm, Morton dips a paw into a jar he’s car-
rying and smears a fistful of peanut butter on
the royal walls.
“Rarrr!”  Morton growls. “I haven’t had so
much fun in weeks!” In one hand, he holds a
ruby-tipped wand. He scrapes it against the
royal blackboard. Luigi claps his hands over
his ears to block out the horrible sound.
"Yikes!" Morton blurts, as he notices Luigi.
"Time  to go!” He darts toward an open win-
dow. Luigi lunges at the evil turtle.
