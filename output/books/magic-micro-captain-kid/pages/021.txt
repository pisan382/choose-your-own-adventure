You can see the captain is looking nerv­
ous, too. Maybe he’s afraid he’s going to
have a mutiny on his hands. “We must fight
magic with magic. Anyone who knows a
spell to open the trunk will get first pick
of the treasure,” he offers.
Immediately, the crewmen get to work!
And they have some ideas of their own.
They try everything from accordion mu­
sic to threats.
You look on in amazement as one pirate
even makes a magic charm — of mashed
bananas and parrot feathers!?! He makes
a big show of spreading it evenly over the
chest and around the lock. Unfortunately,
it doesn’t work. It just makes the chest
sneeze!
Everyone else takes a try at opening the
chest and also fails. Then Captain Robb
turns to you. “What can you do, mate?”
he asks.
That’s a good question, you think to
yourself. And then you hear your Magic
Micro beep. You know it wants your at­
tention.
