Introduction
Would you like to put a little more excitement in your life? You
can become a private eye and solve all kinds of mysteries while
using the computer.
Don't worry if you are not sure about some of the things you will
be doing. THE MYSTERY AND ADVENTURE COMPUTER
STORYBOOK
will
show
you
step-by-step
how
to
enter
the
programs onto your computer and help solve a mystery or follow
an adventure.
THE MYSTERY AND ADVENTURE COMPUTER STORY
BOOK contains twenty-two stories.
Within each story there are
computer programs. You will be asked to type the programs into
your computer and then answer some questions. The computer will
"talk back" and respond to your answers. Many of the computer's
answers will surprise and amuse you.
We hope that you have lots of fun with THE MYSTERY AND
ADVENTURE COMPUTER STORYBOOK.
