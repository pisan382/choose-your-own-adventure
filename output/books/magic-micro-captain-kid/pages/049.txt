Lightning flashes on the screen.
Show it to the turtles.
You hold up the computer and turn in a
circle. The advancing creatures stop in
their tracks. But they’re still snapping.
Maybe it has to flash faster, the Magic
Micro says. Can you enter a number that
will make the program run faster?
When you do, the turtles stop snapping
and draw into their shells. Once again they
look like a harmless circle of alphabet
rocks. But who knows how long they’ll be
out of commission? You hurry across the
beach. Soon you’re off the white sand and
into the forest.
