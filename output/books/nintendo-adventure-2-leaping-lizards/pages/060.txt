As soon as Luigi and Toad cross the bridge
into Water Land, the trees and valleys of the
Mushroom Kingdom vanish. There’s water as
far as the eye can see, dotted with islands that
are linked by rickety wooden bridges.
In the Mushroom Kingdom, Luigi often has
to battle Koopa Troopas or Goomba mush-
rooms. There are none of those here. Instead,
the air is full of Cheep Cheeps-vicious flying
fish with crab-like claws.
