“Now, why didn’t I think of that,” the
captain says, reading the message.
Before you can make a move, he’s
crawled across the deck to one of the life­
boats. You’re surprised at how fast he can
move. As you watch, he cuts the ropes and
the little boat splashes into the water. At
the sound of the splash, the men turn
around. But they’re too late. The captain
has already tossed his fancy hat onto the
deck and done a perfect dive into the water.
In a flash, he pulls himself into the boat
and starts rowing as if his life depended
on it.
“Did you help him get away?” Hal asks,
pointing a pistol at your face.
