Chapter 5
The eats gather around the scene of the
kit-napping. Leo’s mother is trying to be
brave. But you can tell that her eyes are
brimming with tears. The two warriors,
Justinian and Brutus, are all for storming
the castle walls. But the wise Augustus
disagrees.
“We need cunning, not force,’’ Augus­
tus says.
“Yes, we need to get someone into the
queen’s castle,’’ Isis suggests. “Someone
whom she won’t suspect is there to rescue
Leo. Maybe one of us could dress up like
a dog.’’
