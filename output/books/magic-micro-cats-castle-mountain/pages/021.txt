pulled to a stop. The door swings open, and
out steps a tall, pale woman. 1 ler long, dark

The queen turns her dark gaze toward
the mountain. It seems for a moment as if

step back into the shadows.
“I know you’re in there. Come out, you

“Never,” a large gray cat in back of you
mutters. “We’ll never surrender. We don’t
want to be your slaves like all the other
animals in the kingdom.”
