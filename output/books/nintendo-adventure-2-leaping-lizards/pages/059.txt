The Doom Ship sails through the clouds for

forward and heads straight down toward the

“What’s happening?” Luigi blurts out as he
falls against the rail. He grips the Doom Ship’s
rail tightly and peeks over the side.
"Yikes!"  he cries. The bow of the ship is
about to smash into the water!
