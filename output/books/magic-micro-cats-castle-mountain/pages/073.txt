About the program:
The way to make the stars come out is
by knowing your math. The Magic Micro
will give you a series of sums to add up.
Each time you get an answer right, you will
get as many stars as the answer. For ex­
ample, if you answer 11 when the Magic
Micro asks you to add 6 and 5, you will be
right and 11 stars will come out. If you say
12, you will be wrong and you will lose.
The Magic Micro will let you know when
you’ve got enough stars to strengthen the
force field.
